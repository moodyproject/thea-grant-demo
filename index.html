The Experience of Jewelry

Come step inside our bejeweled cabinet of curiosities

Brick and mortar in the heart of the cobblestoned, historical warehouse district of DUMBO, at the foot of the Manhattan Bridge. Thea Grant is more than a jewelry shop -- it is a destination.

Not only a retail shop, we are also jewelers in-the-round, with a workbench in the shop where we offer on-site engraving, customization, re-sizing and repairs. From antique and vintage to custom -- we are partners in life and partners in craft.

"From Antique & Vintage to Custom"

The Craft & The Collection

Antique & Vintage

A curated collection of antique and vintage jewelry -- each piece selected for its character, craftsmanship, and story.

Custom Design

Bespoke jewelry designed and crafted to your vision. From engagement rings to heirloom pieces at our in-shop workbench.

Restoration

Expert repair, re-sizing, and restoration of fine jewelry. We bring old pieces back to life with the respect they deserve.

Customization

Private Appointments

Thursday Nights

La Perla
Pop-Up Bar

Every Thursday evening, the shop transforms. The copper curtain draws back, the lights go warm, and La Perla comes alive -- a pop-up bar in collaboration with Superfine, nestled among the antiques and curiosities.

Cocktails, conversation, and the kind of atmosphere you can only find in a DUMBO warehouse filled with vintage treasures. Jewelry shopping meets speakeasy.
